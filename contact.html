FREQUENTLY ASKED
INFORMATION

Power outlets are 120 volts (same as in the United States).
Alcohol cannot be sold or served between midnight and 9:00 a.m.
The drinking age is 18, though enforcement is not strict.
Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English. In rural areas, especially among older residents, English is spoken very little.
Taniti has one hospital and several clinics. The hospital has many multilingual employees.
Violent crime is rare. However, as tourism increases, pickpocketing and petty crimes are becoming more common.
Taniti has many national holidays. Many attractions and restaurants close during these days, so plan ahead.
Taniti uses the U.S. dollar. Many businesses also accept euros and yen. Several banks offer currency exchange and most places accept major credit cards.
